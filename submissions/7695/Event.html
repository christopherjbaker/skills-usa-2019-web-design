Wedding Information


Our wedding will be taking place in the beautiful city of Papeete, Tahiti on June 14, 2019. Tahiti is absolutely breathtaking and features warm, dry, and sunny weather during the summer months making it the perfect location for our wedding. We would love for you to come celebrate with us!
The wedding ceremony will be taking place in the Notre Dame Cathedral. The reception will be taking place at the beach right next to the Manava Suite Resort Tahiti.
